Who We Are

Agam Ventures is a specialized Accessibility and Digital Publishing solutions provider delivering structured, compliant, and inclusive content for global organizations.

Our expertise spans PDF remediation, document accessibility, alt text authoring, publishing workflows, and compliance validation aligned with WCAG, ADA, Section 508, and PDF/UA standards.
